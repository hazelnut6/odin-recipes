Brownies

Description:

A chocolate brownie, or simply a brownie, is a chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. Brownies often, but not always, have a glossy "skin" on their upper crust.

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Walnuts

Steps:

  1. Mix the ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Spread the batter into a prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry.
  4. Cool, then cut into squares.
